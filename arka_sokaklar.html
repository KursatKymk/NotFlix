Profile

Arka Sokaklar


"Arka Sokaklar" is a long-running Turkish television series about the lives of police officers working at the Istanbul Police Department and crime incidents. The series, which has been broadcast since 2006, caters to a wide audience that includes intra-team relationships, crime solving efforts and the personal lives of the characters.

Profile

Ömer is a young man who lives a simple life by working in his father's workshop. Returning from military service, she plans to marry her lover Eyşan. But when he suddenly finds himself in prison for murder, his life is thrown into turmoil. Uncle Ramiz, whom he met while in prison, becomes his second father.

Eyşan is a beautiful, attractive woman. She is a skilled con artist who has made a career out of manipulating men for their money. She is charming, seductive and ruthless at the same time. She betrayed Omar and became rich and powerful.

Tuncay Kurtiz

Uncle Ramiz, as he is known, is a strong-willed man who came to Istanbul in 1971 and vowed to conquer the city. In the series, he stands out with his intelligence, life experience and the way he prioritizes the rules. He took Ömer under his protection in prison and trained him.

Yiğit Özşener

Cengiz Atay (Yiğit Özşener) is a manipulative man who will do anything for power. He is a talker and believes that he can get out of any situation by talking. He is known for his "Come on" line. He is in love with Eyşan and will do anything for even a single word from her.

Barış Falay

Kerpeten Ali, as he is known, is the tough young man of the neighborhood, living a poor life. He works with Ömer at an auto repair shop. Cengiz used his father, who was in prison because of him, to convince him to betray Ömer.
